# Grocery Discovery Questions

## Your Operation

1. How many store locations do you have?
2. What's your average monthly invoice volume across all locations?
3. Do any vendors split deliveries into multiple invoices?
4. What percentage of your invoices arrive as digital PDFs vs. scanned paper?
5. How many line items does your typical invoice have?
6. How many days from delivery to invoice entry in your accounting system?
7. How many regular vendors do you work with?
8. How often do you add new vendors?

## Your Data

9. How clean is your vendor master?
10. Do your items have UPCs in your POS, with consistent descriptions?
11. How is your chart of accounts structured across locations?
12. How often does your POS pricing data get updated?

## Your Systems

13. What ERP or accounting system are you using?
14. Who manages your accounting system?
15. How do you currently capture invoices that arrive as paper?
16. Does your team have someone comfortable with basic software administration?

## Your Team

17. Who on your team will own the implementation, and how much time can they give it?
18. What does your timeline look like?
19. How will you measure whether this is successful?

## Your Costs

20. How many people are on your AP team?
21. How many department or category managers do you have?
22. Fully-loaded AP hourly rate ($/hr)
23. Minutes per invoice (manual)
24. Current coding practice
25. GM/receiver hours per week on invoice paperwork (per store)
26. GM/store manager hourly rate
27. Dept head hours/week verifying costs
28. Dept head hours/week on invoice admin
29. Department head hourly rate
30. Month-end close overtime hours
31. Monthly reconciliation hours
32. Total annual vendor spend
33. Annual DSD vendor spend
34. Net margin percentage
35. New stores planned (next 12-24 months)
36. Ottimate annual investment
